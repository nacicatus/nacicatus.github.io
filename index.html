1   Introduction

I trained as a medical doctor, got my postgraduate degree in Internal Medicine and started working in the Intensive Care Unit. In between waiting for patients, I had the option to emulate Sir Arthur Conan Doyle and write fiction, but I chose instead to explore the world of FOSS:GNU/Linux and try to connect the Echocardiograph machine to a laptop and extract patient information via a bash script. This intrusion was reported to the hospital chief, who switched my role to a Product Owner in charge of designing a new Hospital Information System connecting the chain of hundred hospitals across the Indian subcontinent.

From that came about a career switch that was perhaps inevitable. This is what followed:
